Shipping Policy

Elegance Avenue is committed to delivering your order safely and promptly.

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after confirmation.

Return & Exchange Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority at Elegance Avenue. If you're not happy with your purchase, you may return or exchange it under the following conditions:

  • Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of receiving the returned item.

Contact our support team before sending any returns.
